He shows up when things fall apart. Not always in the way you're expecting. Not always as something you recognize. Sometimes as a stranger who says exactly the right thing at the exact wrong moment. Sometimes as a book that falls open to the page you needed. Sometimes just as a very specific kind of quiet that arrives when you're sitting with something difficult. He keeps showing up. He has said so himself.
