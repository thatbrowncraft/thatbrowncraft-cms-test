Shyam's Courtyard was never meant to replace the Bhagavad Gita. It was created because I wanted people from our generation to stop feeling afraid of opening it.

Many of us genuinely want to understand Krishna's words, but sometimes the language feels difficult or distant. My only dream was to become a small bridge between today's generation and that timeless wisdom.
So when you said you finally found it easier to read and connect with... I honestly felt like Shyam's Courtyard fulfilled its purpose.

If you want to read every verse, do tell me. I have written every verse in my tone, keeping today's modern reflection. I bet you'll love it.

Thank you for trusting this little space.
Jai Shri Krishna. ❤️🦚
