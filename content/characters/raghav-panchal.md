The eldest Panchal heir and one of Gujarat's sharpest businessmen. Known for his calm mind, ruthless business decisions and commanding presence, he's respected and feared in equal measure. At home, however, he has a completely different side that only his family gets to see. He lovingly calls his wife Idhi and his son Ayan, and despite his serious reputation, those two can make him smile without even trying.
