Anjana is sunshine disguised as a person. Cheerful, affectionate, endlessly optimistic, and hopelessly addicted to fast food, she has a habit of making every room brighter simply by walking into it. Her warmth naturally draws people toward her, and her genuine kindness becomes the heart of every relationship she builds.

Life, however, has a cruel way of changing people.

A single incident transforms the once vibrant woman into someone almost unrecognizable. She becomes quiet, emotionally distant, and painfully reserved, speaking only when necessary while silently carrying burdens no one understands. Yet with every passing year, her grace only deepens. Her calm presence, quiet confidence, and timeless beauty leave a lasting impression on everyone around her, even making her closest friend, Rajveer, quietly fall for the woman she has become.
