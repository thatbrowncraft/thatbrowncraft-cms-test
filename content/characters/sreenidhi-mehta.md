A gifted scientist whose life was turned upside down after betrayal forced her to leave behind the career she loved. Fiercely intelligent, emotionally guarded and quietly resilient, she refuses to let her past define her. Beyond her professional brilliance, she's the heart of the Panchal family. A protective elder sister to Yug, the woman Raghav lovingly calls Idhi, and the devoted mumma of little Idhayan, who is her entire world.
