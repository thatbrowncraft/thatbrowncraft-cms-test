A two year old toddler who firmly believes the Panchal mansion belongs to him. Curious, mischievous and adorably dramatic, he's everyone's favorite tiny human. He's completely obsessed with his Devi Kaki, proudly steals cookies from Aadhu Kaki, refuses to sleep without his Mumma, and follows his Yug Kaka into every questionable adventure. He somehow convinces every adult that whatever chaos he creates is completely justified. Small in size, unlimited in confidence, and unquestionably the heartbeat of the Panchal family.
